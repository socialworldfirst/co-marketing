Co-marketing for clients and brands

For businesses that use WorldFirst. Campaigns in this category cover your business and how it operates internationally, produced by WorldFirst and published by both organisations.

Subject of the campaign

The video covers your business: how you expanded into new markets, how your operations work across countries, and how money moves through the business as part of that. WorldFirst appears where it is part of the operation and not otherwise.

You approve the subject, the final cut and all wording. Short-form versions are supplied for your own channels, and the campaign page can be referenced in your materials and press enquiries.

How this differs from a customer case study

A customer case study is published by a supplier and features the customer endorsing that supplier. A co-marketing campaign is published by both organisations, with both brands presented together, and does not require you to endorse WorldFirst.

The subject is your business rather than our product. If the account of your business would not stand on its own without reference to WorldFirst, the campaign is not correctly scoped.

What is included

01

One video, under five minutes

Horizontal format. Filmed and produced by WorldFirst at our cost. Features named representatives from both organisations. You review and approve the final cut before publication.

02

Short-form versions

Vertical edits taken from the same filming, for use on social channels. Supplied to you for your own channels as well as ours.

03

A campaign page on our website

A dedicated page presenting the collaboration: the video, the story, and both organisations. It remains published after the campaign period and can be linked from your own materials.

04

Distribution

Publication across WorldFirst global social channels, our website and our newsletter. Specific placements are confirmed for each campaign.

What each side contributes

WorldFirst provides

  • Concept, script and production
  • All filming and editing costs
  • The campaign page on our website
  • Distribution to our audience of businesses trading internationally

We ask from you

  • One member of your leadership team on camera
  • Brand assets and approval of the final materials
  • Publication once on your own channels

Examples of this format

Stripe × Amazon

A customer executive describes how the company manages payments across markets, published by the payments provider.
Adyen × Spotify

A customer explains a business problem and how it was addressed, with the supplier not appearing on camera.
Airwallex × McLaren Racing (CFO)

A chief financial officer describes how the organisation manages international payments.

Next step

To discuss a co-marketing campaign, contact your WorldFirst partnership manager or relationship manager.

An initial call takes around 30 minutes and covers the subject of the campaign, who would appear, and the timeline.
