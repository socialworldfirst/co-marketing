Optional, and only where it genuinely applies.

Optional layer

Some partnerships have a commercial engine both sides are already pushing: a card programme where both brands grow when spend grows, a corridor product where both sides win on volume. Where that shared goal exists, the campaign can attach to it directly, with product-level presence, a joint milestone to point at, and a measure both sides agree on.

Where it doesn't, the campaign stands on its own as a story worth telling. Commercial mechanics and any offer for your customers are shaped with your partnership contact, not on this page.
