When we share a business goal

Optional layer, most common with institutions

Where a partnership already has a commercial engine, a card programme, a corridor product, a settlement rail, the campaign can attach to it directly rather than sitting beside it. That means product-level presence for both brands, a joint milestone worth announcing, and a measure both sides recognise.

The clearest public example of this shape is a network and a wallet co-announcing a remittance corridor: no film, no shoot, just a co-brand lockup, two named executives and a genuine product connection. Where a shared goal doesn't exist, the story still stands on its own.
